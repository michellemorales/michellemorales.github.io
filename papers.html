My publications listed below fall into two broad research categories.

The first category is focused on building Machine Learning systems for mental health use cases. Many of my papers which ultimately culminated in my dissertation focused on this research area. Papers in this first category include literature reviews as well as descriptions of methods for building depression classification and suicide risk assessment systems leveraging multimodal datasets (video, audio, and text). Much of the code used to build the systems described in the papers are represented in an open-source tool I built and released as part of my research.

The second category of papers represent the work and research I conducted while at IBM, which focused on applying NLP techniques to assess product competitiveness.
